Most Frequent Questions and Answers

What is the Process of Digital Invitation?

1. Choose a design theme for your invitation.

2. Provide information about the event and upload any pictures you would like to include.

3. Our team of designers will create a digital invite for you within 1-2 days.

4. Once the invite is ready, you will receive a link that you can use to review the invite and make any necessary changes.

5. After you approve the invite, you can share the link with your guests.

With how many people I can share this?

Unlimited, You can share the digital invitation link with an unlimited number of people, and there is no limit on how many times you can share it. You can share the link with all of your guests without any restrictions.

How much time it- takes to make my invitation?

It typically takes our team of designers 48-72 hours to create a digital invitation after you have provided the event details and any desired photos.

However, if your event is coming up soon and you need the invitation quickly, we do offer a 12-hour delivery option for rush orders.

Life of Invitation?

The digital invitation will remain active for one month after your event date. If you would like to keep the invitation live for longer, we can do so for a small annual maintenance fee.

Can I open this Invitation on Desktop/ Laptop and Tab

Our digital invitations are optimized for mobile devices, and a QR code is provided for guests to open the invite on their mobile. Its not made for desktop or laptops.

Where I can share this?

You can share the digital invitation link via WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, email, or any other digital platform.

Is it a video invitation?

No. Its not a video invitation.

Our digital invitations are in the form of a website, not a video.
